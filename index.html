India’s Laws Explained

Veeru is an open access, digital resource that provides simple, actionable, reliable and accessible legal information in multiple formats, helping solve day-to-day legal problems so that citizens are aware of their rights and feel empowered to seek justice.

Legal Explainers

This is Veeru’s repository of legal explainers with simple, actionable, reliable and accessible legal information on a variety of subjects. We have divided them into nine broad themes, so you can easily locate explainers on issues that interest you. Each explainer will help you understand the law that governs that issue and the remedies available to you under that law.

Labour & Employment

You can find legal explainers on topics related to employment and on issues you may face in your job, and your legal rights, such as on wages, maternity benefit, employment contracts etc.

Family & Marriage

You can find legal explainers on topics related to marriage and divorce, based on the personal laws that govern them. You can also find information on types of relationships recognized under the law like live-in relationships.

Money & Property

You can find legal explainers related to personal finance and property, such as bank fraud, cheques, rent etc. You can get an overview of the legal issues that you may face around managing money and property, and the legal remedies available.

Crimes & Violence

You can find legal explainers on different kinds of violence that are directed at specific groups or are sexual crimes or related to property or family. Learn more about your legal remedies against such abuse.

Women laws

Dowry is anything which has value (such as cash and property) which is given by the bride’s family to the groom’s family as a condition of marriage.1 The law does not stop the exchange of gifts with the fiance’s family during the wedding. The purpose of this law is to prohibit coercion during the gift exchange.
